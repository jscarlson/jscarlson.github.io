About

Hi! I'm Jake, an incoming PhD student in the department of economics at Harvard University. I graduated from Yale College in 2020, where I pursued a double major in Statistics and Data Science (S&DS) and Ethics, Politics, and Economics (EP&E).

I currently spend most of my time thinking about how to best harness the power of deep learning methods and models (especially those from computer vision and NLP) to advance empirical economics research.

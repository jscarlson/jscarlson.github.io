Calcul8R

Calcul8R is a graphing calculator I developed with natural mathematical display. It is currently available for mobile iOS devices.

Functionally, it is rather similar to a TI-83 or TI-84 graphing calculator, which is the current standard for graphing calculators in most U.S. high schools. Moreover, these same calculators are nationally supported for SAT and ACT testing, making their interface especially comfortable to use for most high school students.
